Location

Thanks for stopping by. We are the last authentic Indian restaurant in Canada, serving delicious Indian cuisine cooked by the best chefs. It only takes a few minutes to browse through our website and check out our menu. We hope you'll soon join us for a superb culinary experience.

A Unique Experience

Our menu was carefully assembled to offer a genuine Indian restaurant in Ottawa taste experience. Dishes range from spicy to sweet and flavors from exotic to amazing. Let your eyes take part in the experience and look through our menu – it is all part of the Indian experience!


Cuisine

Born out of Pure Love for Friends Food and Family. We serve the best of Indian delicacies for the foodie’s delight. Traditional folk music playing in the background while you enjoy your delicious meal makes for a captivating experience.

Take Out Menu

You don’t feel like cooking tonight or you have no time to do so? Avoid junk food! Pre-order tasty, healthy, flavourful Indian restaurant food made to your liking! Enjoy it in the comfort of your own home!
